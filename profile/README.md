AI systems hallucinate with complete confidence. Every session opens at zero. Contradictions get smoothed over in good prose.

The missing piece isn't capability — it's **epistemics**. The ability to know what you know, trace your reasoning, and recognize when you're wrong.

We build systems that have epistemics:
- **Memory that fades** without reinforcement
- **Knowledge that requires evidence** — verified sources, not confident assertions
- **Provenance you can trace** — an unbroken line from observation to belief
- **Contradictions that surface** — detected on write, not discovered later
